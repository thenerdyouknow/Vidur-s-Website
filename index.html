About Me

Hi!

My name is Vidur, aka blue heart beat.

I started my journey of music at the age of 6, when I was gifted a small toy piano. Since then, I've come a long way.

I began singing and writing songs in 11th grade. That's when the dream started. The dream of actually making it, as a musician in India. The dream of being successful.

I've travelled around the world with my ukulele/guitar, trying to spread my music, and to make enough money to record my songs. My first album, consisting of 10 songs is due to be out by the end of 2019.

If you're here, you probably saw me playing on the streets somewhere. The response I've gotten on the streets has been mind blowing. From being told that I was "disgracing" my family, to being told it was a privilege listening to me, I've seen it all. And all I can say is thank you.

Thank you for all the love and support. It means more than I can say. I know I'm going to make my dream come true and I hope, sincerely, that you stick around to see it happen!

Love,

Vidur Singh
